Apache nifi FAQs

  • How do you pronounce NiFi?

    "nye fye" (nī fī) is the preferred pronunciation.

  • Why is no one replying to my mailing list question?

    The community makes every effort to respond to all mailing list questions. While there are contributors across the world, some timezones are more active than others, so you should allow at least 24 hours (longer on weekends) to get a response. In addition, make sure you are subscribed to the mailing list you're contacting, as replies go back to the list. If you do not subscribe, t can appear as if no one is answering, when in fact there has been a response. Additional information on Apache mailing lists is available here.
